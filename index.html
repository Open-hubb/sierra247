Who We Are

Your Safety,
Our Priority

Sierra 24/7 Securicor and Logistics Services SL Ltd is a professional security and multi-service company headquartered in Freetown, Sierra Leone. Founded on January 1, 2023, we have rapidly established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses and individuals seeking comprehensive protection and operational support.

Our business is unique because we go beyond conventional security. We provide professionally trained security guards to our clients while ensuring that every aspect of your safety and operational needs is met with the highest standards of excellence.
